About Me

My name is Justin Clark and I am from Plainville, MA. I am in the process of earning a bachelors degree in business administration with a concentration in business operations at Babson College. While attending college, I am also a part of the Babson club soccer team. I have played soccer my whole life and it is one of my passions, so last summer my brother and I started a coaching program that provided one on one training to rising high school freshmen within our area. We worked on fitness, technical skills, strategy and overall fundamentals to try and prepare these kids as they prepared for higher level soccer. We have continued to slowly grow and will be continuing this summer. If I am not playing soccer, I will most likely be watching a movie or solving rubiks cubes.

I chose Babson College because it seemed like it provided the most real life experience out of any of the other colleges I was considering. Additionally, Babson does not force one subject down your throat, but instead immerses you in all aspects of business. FME was a big selling point when deciding to come here, and I was even more impressed when it came time to acutally undertake the FME project. I chose to concentrate in operations because I feel as though it is a versatile skill that can be used across any business segment, and it allows for creativity in the workplace. After graduation, I hope to be offered a full time position at my current company, and continue to work within the business operations department.
